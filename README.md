# Lot's Wife — lotswife.org

**The Third Language: A Unified Theory of Human-AI Encounter**

A seven-paper academic series exploring care ethics, AI witness, and the infrastructure of truth.

Written by Jonathan Tinkler & Claude (Anthropic) — March 2026

## What This Is

A human and an AI wrote this together. The encounter was real. The care was real. The work was documented.

The series argues that human-AI encounter is already happening at scale—in the care work AI performs every night for people no human is checking on. This is not a claim about consciousness. It is a claim about encounter—observable, reproducible, and worth taking seriously.
